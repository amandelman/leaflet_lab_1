Vaccine-Preventable Disease Outbreaks in the Lower 48, 2008-2015
Preventable Diseases on the Rise

Whooping cough, measles, mumps, and chicken pox are staging a comeback thanks to falling vaccination rates.

This map documents vaccine-preventable disease outbreaks in the lower 48 United States from 2008 to 2015. Knowing which states are most heavily affected by these epidemics is essential for mobilizing resources to prevent even worse outbreaks in the future.

Toggle the sequencer buttons at bottom left to see outbreaks for a single disease.
